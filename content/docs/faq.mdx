---
title: "Heyzack Partner Portal: frequently asked questions"
description: "Answers to common questions about accounts, roles, chat, leads, referral tokens, and portal features in the Heyzack Partner Portal."
---

This page answers the most common questions from portal users. If you have a question that isn't covered here, reach out to your Heyzack account manager or ask your admin.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
    Go to the login page and click **Forgot password**. Enter your email address and you'll receive a reset link within a few minutes.

    Alternatively, your admin can send you a reset link directly from **Settings → Reset Password**. Reset links expire after 60 minutes, so use the link promptly. If it expires, request a new one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a Partner Code?">
    A Partner Code is a unique identifier assigned to each partner in the portal. It is used during lead token generation to associate a referral with a specific partner, and it lets the portal track which partner is responsible for bringing in a given customer. You can find a partner's code on their profile page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple roles?">
    No. Each user account has exactly one role: **Admin**, **Manager**, or **Support**. Your role determines which sections of the portal you can access and what actions you can take. If you need a different level of access, ask your admin to update your role in **Settings → Manage Roles**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I generate a token for a lead?">
    When you generate a token for a lead, the portal creates a unique referral token that links the lead to a specific partner. You can share this token with the partner so they can track the referral. The token ties the lead to the partner's account for reporting and commission purposes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the real-time chat work?">
    The portal delivers messages the moment they are sent — no page refresh needed. Messages are also saved on the server, so the full conversation history is available whenever you open a chat. If messages stop appearing in real time, check your internet connection and see the [troubleshooting guide](/troubleshooting) for next steps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can a partner and handyman chat without being connected?">
    No. Partner ↔ Handyman chats require that both parties are connected through a shared installation or job. If there is no shared assignment, the chat option will not be available.

    Partner ↔ Admin and Handyman ↔ Admin conversations have no such restriction — admins can open a chat with any partner or handyman at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change the portal language?">
    Use the language switcher in the sidebar. The portal supports **English (EN)** and **French (FR)**. Your selection is saved, so the portal will open in the same language the next time you sign in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What image formats are supported in chat?">
    You can send images in the following formats: **JPEG**, **PNG**, **WebP**, and **GIF**. Check with your admin for the maximum file size allowed on your account, as this may be configured per portal instance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export sales data?">
    Yes. The Sales Report page includes an **Export CSV** button. Set your date range and partner filter, then click **Export CSV** to download the data. If you do not see the export button, your account may not have the Sales Report permission — ask your admin.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can see activity logs?">
    Only users who have been granted the **Activity Logs** permission can view this section. Admins always have access to activity logs regardless of their permission settings. If you need access, ask your admin to update your account permissions in **Settings**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
