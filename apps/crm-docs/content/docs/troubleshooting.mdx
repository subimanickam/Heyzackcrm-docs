This page covers the most frequently reported issues in the Heyzack Partner Portal and how to resolve them. Work through the steps for your issue before contacting support — most problems can be resolved without raising a ticket.

## Authentication problems

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't log in">
    Start with the basics before assuming a deeper problem:

    1. Double-check that you're entering the correct email address for your account.
    2. Re-enter your password carefully — check that **Caps Lock** is off, as passwords are case-sensitive.
    3. If you've forgotten your password, click **Forgot password** on the login page and follow the reset instructions. Reset links expire after 60 minutes.
    4. If none of the above works, ask your admin to verify that your account is active in **Settings → Manage Roles**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I keep getting logged out">
    The portal uses session tokens that expire after a period of inactivity. When your session expires, you are automatically signed out for security. Simply sign in again to resume your work.

    If you are being logged out unexpectedly during an active session, try clearing your browser cookies and signing back in. If the problem persists, report it to your admin.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I get a 403 error on API calls">
    A `403 Forbidden` error means the portal's API rejected your request because your account does not have permission to perform that action or access that resource.

    Ask your admin to review your role and permission settings in **Settings → Manage Roles**. Once the correct permissions are granted, try the action again.

    <Warning>
      Do not attempt to work around 403 errors by modifying request headers or tokens. Access controls exist to protect your organisation's data.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Chat problems

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Messages aren't appearing in real time">
    The portal uses a real-time messaging service for live message delivery. If messages are delayed or not appearing at all:

    1. Check your internet connection — a dropped connection will interrupt the real-time feed.
    2. Refresh the page. The chat will reconnect and load any messages you missed.
    3. If you are on a corporate or managed network, ask your IT team to confirm that the portal's real-time messaging service is not blocked by a firewall or web proxy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can't start a chat with a handyman">
    Partner ↔ Handyman chats are only available when both parties share a connected installation or job. If you do not have a shared assignment, the chat option will not appear.

    Contact your admin to verify the connection or to create the appropriate installation assignment before attempting to chat.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I see duplicate messages">
    Duplicate messages can appear on slow or unstable connections when a message is retried before the first delivery is confirmed. This is a known edge case.

    Refresh the page to clear the duplicates. The server stores only one copy of each message, so the conversation history will be correct after a refresh.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Installations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't find an installation">
    If an installation doesn't appear in your list:

    1. Use the **search bar** at the top of the Installations page and search by customer name, address, or installation ID.
    2. Check the status filter — set it to **All Status** to make sure you're not filtering out the installation you're looking for.
    3. The installation may have been assigned to a different partner. Ask your admin to search across all partners and reassign if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The status won't change">
    If you cannot update an installation's status:

    1. Confirm that your account has the **Installations** permission. Without it, status fields are read-only.
    2. Check that the installation is in a state that allows a transition — some statuses can only be set from specific preceding statuses.
    3. If you have the permission and the transition should be valid, contact your admin. They can update the status directly or investigate any underlying data issues.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Leads

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The Generate Token button is greyed out">
    The **Generate Token** button is only active when a lead meets both of the following conditions:

    - The lead has a valid ID assigned by the system.
    - The lead is in an eligible status (for example, **Qualified**).

    If the button is still greyed out after confirming both conditions, refresh the page and try again. If the problem continues, contact your admin.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A converted lead still shows as Qualified">
    Status changes sometimes take a moment to propagate across the portal. Refresh the page — the updated status should appear after a reload.

    If the lead still shows the wrong status after refreshing, ask your admin to check the lead record directly and correct the status manually if needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A menu item is missing">
    If you cannot see a section of the portal that you expect to access, your account has not been granted the permission for that menu item. Menu-level access is controlled per user in **Settings → Create User** (set at account creation) or by recreating the account with the correct permissions.

    Contact your admin and ask them to enable the relevant permission for your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The page is loading slowly">
    Try the following steps in order:

    1. **Clear your browser cache** — outdated cached assets can slow down page loads. Clear the cache and reload.
    2. **Try a different browser** — if the page loads faster in another browser, the issue may be browser-specific.
    3. **Check your internet connection** — run a speed test to rule out a network issue on your end.

    <Tip>
      Chrome and Edge tend to perform best with the portal. If you're using an older browser, consider upgrading to the latest version.
    </Tip>

    If the portal is consistently slow across browsers and connections, the issue may be on the server side. Report it to your admin so they can check the platform status.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  If your issue isn't listed here, contact your Heyzack account manager for further assistance.
</Note>
