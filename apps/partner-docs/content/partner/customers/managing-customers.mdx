---
title: "View customer records and installation history"
description: "How to open customer records, review full contact details, inspect timelines, and track installation history."
---

Use the **Customers** section to open existing customer records and review everything linked to each account, including contact information, activity timeline, and installation history.

## Open a customer record

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Customers">
    Open **Customers** from the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the customer">
    Use search, status filters, or pagination to locate the record you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open details">
    Click the row (or **View**) to open the full customer detail page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## View customer details

Click anywhere on a customer's row in list view, or click the **View** button, to open the customer detail page. This page is divided into four tabs:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Details" icon="address-card">
    Full contact information — name, email, phone, and address — plus account metadata including the creation date and last updated date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Installations" icon="screwdriver-wrench">
    Every installation job linked to this customer, including the job title, current status, scheduled date, and any completion photos uploaded by your handymen.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Timeline" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    A chronological activity log showing key events across the customer's lifecycle.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact" icon="phone">
    Quick-access contact actions — view the customer's phone number or send them an email directly from the portal.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## View a customer's installation history

Open the customer detail page and select the **Installations** tab. Each installation entry shows:

- **Title** — the name of the installation job
- **Status** — the current stage of the installation (for example, Scheduled, Started, or Completed)
- **Scheduled date** — when the job is planned
- **Completion photos** — thumbnails of photos uploaded at job completion; click any thumbnail to open the full-size image viewer

<Tip>
  If a customer has more than two completion photos on a single installation, a **+N** button appears next to the thumbnails. Click it to open the photo viewer and browse all images.
</Tip>

## Customer statuses

A customer's status reflects their current relationship with your business:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Active** | The customer has an open or recently completed engagement. |
| **Inactive** | The customer's engagement has ended or been paused. Inactive records remain in your database and can be searched or reactivated. |

## Search and pagination

The search bar on the Customers page matches against name, email, and phone number simultaneously. Filtered results respect the current status filter if one is applied.

The list displays **10 customers per page**. Use the **Previous** and **Next** buttons — or click a specific page number — to move through the full dataset. If you search or change filters while on a later page, the view resets to page 1 so you always see the most relevant results first.
