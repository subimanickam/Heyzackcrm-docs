Ordering devices through the HeyZack Partner Portal is a two-step process: you shop on the HeyZack Shopify storefront and then monitor each order's progress from the Orders page. This guide walks you through both steps, including how to apply your available partner credit to reduce the amount due at checkout.

## Place an order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the storefront">
    Go to the **Orders** page and click **Shop Now** in the top-right corner. The HeyZack storefront opens in a new tab. You are signed in automatically — no separate login is required.

    <Note>
      SSO signs you in using your Partner Portal credentials. If the storefront does not load correctly, refresh the tab and try again.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and add items to your cart">
    Browse the available smart home products — Door Lock, Security Camera, Motion Sensor, Smart Home Hub, Window Sensor, and Smart Doorbell — and add the items and quantities you need to your cart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply partner credit at checkout (optional)">
    Before completing checkout, generate a discount code from the Dashboard (see [Using partner credit](#using-partner-credit) below) and paste it into the discount code field in your Shopify cart. The credit amount is deducted from your order total automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Review your order summary and confirm the purchase. Your order will appear on the **Orders** page shortly after it is processed, with the status **Pending**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Track an order

All orders you place through the storefront are visible on the **Orders** page. To check the status of a specific order:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Orders page">
    Navigate to **Orders** in the left-hand sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your order">
    Use the search bar to enter the order ID or item name, or use the status filter to narrow the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View order details">
    Click **View Details** on any order row to open a panel showing the full order information: order ID, item name, quantity, total amount, ordered date, and — if the order has been delivered — the delivery date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Order status lifecycle

Orders move through the following stages:

```
Pending → Approved → Delivered
              ↓
          Cancelled
```

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Order submitted; waiting for payment confirmation |
| **Approved** | Payment confirmed; order is being prepared for shipment |
| **Delivered** | Order fulfilled and shipped to you |
| **Cancelled** | Order voided, refunded, or cancelled before fulfilment |

<Warning>
  Once an order reaches **Delivered** status it cannot be modified. Contact your HeyZack account manager if you have a delivery issue or need to initiate a return.
</Warning>

## Using partner credit

Partner credit is a balance the portal applies as a discount against your Shopify order total. You generate a coupon code from the Dashboard and paste it at checkout.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Dashboard">
    Navigate to the **Dashboard** in the left-hand sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Available Credit card">
    Locate the **Available Credit** card. It shows your current credit balance in EUR. Click it to open the credit application panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your cart total and credit amount">
    Enter the total value of your Shopify cart and the amount of credit you want to apply. The portal calculates the remaining amount due after the credit is deducted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the coupon code">
    Click **Apply Credit**. The system generates a discount coupon code tied to your account and displays it on screen.

    <Tip>
      Copy the coupon code before leaving the Dashboard — it is displayed only once per request.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue to checkout">
    Click **Continue to checkout**. This opens your Shopify cart. Paste the coupon code into the discount field and complete your purchase. The credit amount is applied immediately to reduce the total due.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Partner credit reflects your tier discount and any promotional credits added by HeyZack. Your available balance updates after each order is processed.
</Note>
